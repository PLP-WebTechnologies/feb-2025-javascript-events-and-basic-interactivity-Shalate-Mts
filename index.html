Baking for Beginners

The perfect place to start your sweet journey into the world of baking! Whether you're looking to make your first batch of cookies or try a simple loaf of bread, this page offers easy-to-follow recipes, tips, and tricks that anyone can master. With straightforward instructions and minimal ingredients, you'll be whipping up delicious treats in no time. No fancy tools or experience required—just a love for good food and a little bit of fun!

Easy recipes to start your journey

Chocolate Chip Cookies Banana Bread
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
Ingredients:
  • 2 ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup walnuts (optional)
Instructions:
  • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C)
  • Mix wet ingredients together till smooth.
  • Add all dry ingredients and mix well.
  • Scoop dough onto a baking sheet.
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden.
  • Cool and enjoy!
Instructions:
  • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Mash the bananas and mix with sugar and egg.
  • Add flour and baking soda and stir till combined.
  • Grease pan and pur dough.
  • Bake for 50-60 minutes.
  • Cool and slice!
